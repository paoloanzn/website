# False Idols

If you're between 18 and 30, don't forget: the goal is money. Make a lot of it. Make it fast. Make it now before the AI edge is gone, before you have time to stop, before anyone asks you how you're doing.

What? Why should you?

What a stupid question. What could possibly matter more than making the most money possible? What else should fill your mind? What else is _safe_ to fill it with? Don't you know God (Money) is listening to you right now?

He will have his revenge. He will let you live out your days as a broken, broke, failed pleb just like the others. Have you seen them? Laughing on their lunch breaks. Calling their mothers. Going on walks with people whose net worth they don't know. Pitiable. I genuinely feel bad for them.

How could anyone ever love you otherwise? Do these people really believe they deserve to be loved _for who they are_? Pure insanity. Love is a payout like any other: you earn it. Anyone with even a residue of intellect knows: you are worth exactly your achievements. Nothing more. Nothing more is even wanted.

![Money is God](https://i.postimg.cc/BbL3twmB/Chat-GPT-Image-Apr-22-2026-10-52-25-AM.png)

Do they really think they can be better than someone with more money than them? How?

And then, at the end, when we, the glorious warriors who endured the struggle, finally emerge as winners, they'll be there. They'll ask, "How did you do it?" And finally, _finally_, we will be seen. (Not that we needed it. We don't need anything. But isn't it obvious? I took on pain. Isolation. A permanent sense of inadequacy. A vague, humming dread whenever the apartment is quiet. It was worth it. Obviously.)

Yes, we may have lost people on the way up. How can you ascend without cutting out the weeds? My best friend used to call. He had a girlfriend, a hobby, opinions about movies, he was holding me back with his balance. I stopped picking up. How could I elevate while surrounded by people who don't think exclusively about making the most money possible? How do they even look at themselves in the mirror? I don't. I haven't in a while. There's nothing useful in there.

Fine. We'll stay at the top alone. People say the top is lonely. It's not lonely, there's the work, the dashboards, the notifications, the grind. The work is company enough. At night sometimes I hear my heartbeat very clearly, like it's in a large empty room, but this is fine. This is normal. This is what winning sounds like.

Everything for the goal. The only goal. The divine purpose. Our entire life and identity.

_May my life be filled with success, money, and achievement._ _May there be no other purpose outside of this._ _May I never have to find out who I am when I stop._ _May the lever never break._ _May the silence never find me._

Amen.
